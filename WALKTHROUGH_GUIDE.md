## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

**Pointer won't lock?**
- Make sure you clicked inside the 3D view
- Try clicking again

**Can't move?**
- Ensure pointer is locked (click first)
- Check that you're using WASD or arrow keys

**Controls not showing?**
- Click the "Controls" button in bottom-left
- Panel may be hidden

**Room labels not appearing?**
- Make sure you're walking through doorways
- Labels only show when changing rooms

**Too slow/fast?**
- Use SHIFT to sprint (2x speed)
- Normal speed is realistic walking pace
